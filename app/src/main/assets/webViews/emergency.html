Emergency contacts

In crisis now!

If you or the person you are concerned about is in immediate danger of hurting themselves or someone else call:

999 - off campus

+44 (0)1904 32 3333* on campus or use the  Safezone app

Samaritans - +44 (0)8457 90 90 90
Someone to talk to in confidence 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Crisis and Access Service York - +44 (0) 1904 610700
Assess individuals experiencing a mental health crisis 24/7.

York Hospital Accident and Emergency - +44(0)1904 631313
Trained to deal with mental health crises and can access specialist support 24/7.
