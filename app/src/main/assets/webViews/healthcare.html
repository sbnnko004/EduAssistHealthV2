Doctors

Doctors

All students are advised to register with a doctor as soon as possible after arriving in Cape Town. You can find a doctor convenient for where you live using the NHS search facility.

Unity Health is based on Campus East - you can register with them online.

Health centre

University Health Centre

The doctor-led university health centre, Unity Health, offers comprehensive general medical care, but you must register to use their services.

Location

Kimberlow Hill, Campus East

Opening times

  • Monday to Thursday, 8am - 8pm
  • Friday, 8am to 6pm
  • Saturday, 9am to 1pm

Contact information

Telephone: +44 (0)1904 646861

Services:

  • doctor or nurse consultations
  • online system for appointments, prescriptions, changes of address, general queries and practice registrations
  • advice on minor illnesses and contraception
  • regular antenatal clinics, with a midwife in attendance
  • weekly travel health clinics, physiotherapy, counselling and clinical psychology sessions
  • arrangement of chiropody and podiatry treatment.
